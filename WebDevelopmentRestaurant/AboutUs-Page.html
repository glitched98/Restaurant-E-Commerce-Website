About Us
History:

Lavish's Eatery's history began centuries ago, even before humans began life on this earth. When the predators that roamed this world hunted for fresh meat to feast on. Packed with nutrients, vitamins and protein. The perfect source of food, rich in beneficial minerals. Lavish's Eatery stands on these fundemental aspects of food, the freshness of pure protein. With freshly cut, non-frozen meat used everyday to serve all of our customers with the best there is.


From the delicate blend of spicies imported from all over the world to the fresh local meat provided by our own farmers. Lavish's Eatery provides unrivalled mouth watering flavour that would leave any customer hungry for more. Our founding father, Dr.Gummy Bear, was a perfectionist who understood the delicate blend of spicies used to provide the human body with unparalleled taste. Dr.Gummy Bear, was known to be a culinary genius, inventing new mixes with fresh ingredients that would make any king in any land abandon their amries just for a taste of his creation.


We, at Lavish's Eatery, strive to follow in our founding father's footsteps and achieve the tastes only dreamt of by any common human. Today, you can find Lavish's Eatery in 6 different continents, with each branch presenting its customers with a unique flavorful experience. Whether this is your first time for millionth time visiting us, our staff is excited to welcome you to the taste of your life.
