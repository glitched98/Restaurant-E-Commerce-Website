Beef Stew

DESCRIPTION

This combination of beef, carrots, and potatos makes a stew you will never forget

INGREDIENTS

2lb of cubed beef stew meat
3 tablespoons of vegetable oil
4 cubes of beef bouillon, crumbled
4 teaspoons of cold water
1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
1 teaspoon of dried parsley
2 teaspoon of ground black pepper
3 large potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
4 carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces
4 stalks of celery, cut into 1 inch pieces
1 large onion
2 teaspoons of cornstarch
